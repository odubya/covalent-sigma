Project
FWA Analysis
Description

FWA accounts for 5-10% of healthcare costs. Undetected billing and practice deficiencies cause large fines; penalties can be severe to a reputation as well. Multi-party participation over time creates difficulty in finding true-positive insights.

This project analytically discovers practice or pricing inefficiencies, dispensing errors or inconsistencies between orders and actual stock. Predict fraud, increase visibility into waste and abuse, promote information-driven best practice policies and procedures.
